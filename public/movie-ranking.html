The Best Movies According To Sabih

My top three movies of all time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

J.R.R. Tolkien's epic fantasy saga follows the journey of a humble hobbit, Frodo Baggins, as he and a fellowship of diverse companions embark on a perilous quest to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth from the dark lord Sauron's tyranny.

The Hobbit

In J.R.R. Tolkien's enchanting prelude to "The Lord of the Rings," Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant hobbit hero, joins a band of dwarves led by the legendary warrior Thorin Oakenshield on a grand adventure to reclaim their homeland from the fearsome dragon Smaug.

Kung Fu Panda

DreamWorks Animation's delightful tale follows Po, a clumsy yet determined panda who dreams of becoming a kung fu master. When an ancient prophecy selects him as the Dragon Warrior, Po must train with the Furious Five to protect the Valley of Peace from the villainous Tai Lung and discover the true meaning of heroism.
